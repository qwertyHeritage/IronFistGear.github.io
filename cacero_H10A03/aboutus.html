About Iron Fist Gear

Our Purpose and Goal

Our purpose at Iron Fist Gear is to provide martial artist and gamers with everything they need in one convenient place, from reliable martial arts equipment to popular video games and official merchandise. We are dedicated to supporting athletes in their training, connecting gamers with the culture of fighting and building a community where passion for competition thrives.

Our Business

Iron Fist Gear began as a small online store in 2023, aiming to bridge the gap between high-quality combat sports training and the thrilling world of fighting video games. We opened our first store in Ottawa and we offer durable gear, controllers, and community focused events.

We are a customer first company, committed to providing gear that stands up to the toughest training sessions and controllers that give you the competitive edge in every match.
